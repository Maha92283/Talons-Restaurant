Overview:

Talons has been a cornerstone of Vancouverâ€™s culinary scene since its inception in 1998. Nestled in the heart of the city, the restaurant captures the essence of the Pacific Northwest, blending fresh, locally-sourced ingredients with innovative cooking techniques.

The name "Talons" reflects the restaurant's commitment to precision and craftsmanship, akin to a bird of prey expertly navigating its environment.


Founders:

Talons was founded by renowned chef Anna Moretti and her partner, sommelier David Chen. With a shared passion for culinary artistry and fine dining, they envisioned a space that combined sophisticated flavors with a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Chef Moretti, who trained in Europe and honed her skills in some of the finest kitchens, sought to create a menu that celebrated both the bounty of the region and the artistry of contemporary cuisine.


Philosophy:

At Talons, the philosophy revolves around sustainability and seasonality. The menu changes frequently to reflect the best available ingredients, emphasizing local farmers, fishermen, and artisans.

This commitment to sustainability has earned Talons a loyal following among both locals and visitors seeking an authentic Vancouver dining experience.


Ambiance:

The restaurant features a warm, contemporary design with natural elements that evoke the beauty of the Pacific Northwest. Soft lighting, elegant table settings, and artwork inspired by the region create a cozy yet sophisticated dining environment, perfect for both intimate dinners and celebratory gatherings.


Accolades:

Since its opening, Talons has received numerous awards and accolades, including recognition from the Michelin Guide, which awarded it its first star shortly after launching its seasonal tasting menu.

The restaurant has also been featured in prominent food and travel publications, further solidifying its status as a must-visit destination in Vancouver.


Legacy:

Over the years, Talons has continued to evolve, welcoming guest chefs and incorporating global influences while remaining true to its roots. The restaurant has become a beloved institution, known for its exceptional cuisine, impeccable service, and commitment to sustainability.

As it celebrates over two decades of excellence, Talons remains a culinary beacon in Vancouver, attracting food enthusiasts from around the world.
